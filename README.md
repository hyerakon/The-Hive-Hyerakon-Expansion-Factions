# Faction Warfare Expansion - Custom Server Module for DayZ

## Overview

**Faction Warfare Expansion** is a custom faction system module built on top of the DayZ Expansion Bundle, designed exclusively for private servers focused on roleplay, faction conflict, and emergent gameplay.

This mod introduces structured factions with predefined alliances, rivalries, and special roles to foster constant tension and meaningful player interactions.

## Core Concept

The world is no longer neutral.  
Every survivor belongs to a faction.  
Every faction has allies, enemies, and consequences.

Key features include:

- Defined faction relationships  
- Staff-controlled factions  
- Wanted states  
- Elite and high-threat entities  

## Factions

### Regular Factions

- **Survivor (Green)**  
  Base faction, neutral by default and non-hostile to other regular factions.

- **Blue Brigade (Blue)**  
  Organized and disciplined. Enemies of Red Legion; friendly to Survivors and Yellow Helmets.

- **Red Legion (Red)**  
  Militant and relentless. Enemies of Blue Brigade; friendly to Survivors and Yellow Helmets.

- **Yellow Helmets (Yellow)**  
  Staff faction acting as overseers, mediators, and enforcers. Friendly to all factions.

- **Parasites**  
  Corrupted hostile force hostile to everyone with unique and dangerous abilities.

### Additional & Special Factions

- **Wanted – Survivor**  
  Rogue individuals hostile toward everyone.

- **Wanted – Blue Brigade**  
  Marked criminals friendly only to Blue Brigade.

- **Wanted – Red Legion**  
  Marked criminals friendly only to Red Legion.

- **Yellow Helmet Elite**  
  Elite punitive unit of Yellow Helmets. Ruthless and uncompromising.

- **Parasite Elite**  
  Apex-level threat. Extremely rare and deadly. If you see one… run.

## Installation

1. Ensure you have the required DayZ Expansion modules installed and licensed.  
2. Copy this mod’s files into your server’s mod folder.  
3. Add the mod to your server startup parameters.  
4. Configure faction settings according to your server’s lore and rules.  
5. Restart the server and verify faction functionality.
